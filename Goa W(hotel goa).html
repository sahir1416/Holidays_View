W Hotel, Goa

Vagator Beach, Bardez, 403507 Vagator, India

Price:- 5,000₹-7,000₹ /per night

Description:-


Located along Juhu Beach, JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu boasts 3 outdoor pools, the award-winning Quan Spa and 6 dining options including a bakery. Guestrooms are luxurious and offer breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea.


The refurbished rooms at the 5-star JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu feature fine beddings and furnishings with a pillow menu. They have both cable and satellite TV, while en suite bathrooms come with large mirrors and a bathtub.


Lotus Café overlooking the blooming lotus pond and the Arabian Sea is a 24-hour dining restaurant offering a variety of Indian and International delicacies along with a sumptuous buffet spread. Guests can relish an array of exotic freshly baked homemade breads, sandwiches, cakes, pastries, lavish breakfast dishes and festive treats from all around the world at Bombay Baking Company. Dashanzi offers Asian cuisine and uniquely crafted cocktails, while Saffron is the fine dining Indian restaurant that specialises in traditional royal recipes. Guests can enjoy Italian flavours at Mezzo Mezzo or a drink at Reflections - the sea-facing lobby lounge and bar.


Guests can enjoy free fitness classes or be pampered with indulgent massages and body treatments at the spa. A salt water pool is also offered. Alternatively, the hotel’s tour desk helps guests organise excursions to local attractions.


JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu is within walking distance of Juhu Beach and various popular eateries and shopping outlets. The hotel provides easy access to the business district and is approximately 4.8 km away from Chhatrapati Shivaji Airport.


Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.1 for a two-person trip.
